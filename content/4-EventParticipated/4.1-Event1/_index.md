# Event Report

### Key Topics

#### Automatic Prompt Engineering: Improving the Output Quality of Large Language Models

* **Speaker:** Thịnh Nguyễn

#### Transitioning to Modern Application Architecture – Microservices Architecture

### Key Takeaways

This event provided valuable insights into **Prompt Engineering** and its role in maximizing the performance of Large Language Models (LLMs). One of the key lessons was that AI systems produce better results when users provide clear, structured, and well-defined prompts.

The speaker emphasized that **Prompt Engineering is a systematic discipline rather than a trial-and-error process**. Effective prompts require logical thinking, contextual understanding, and continuous refinement to obtain accurate and reliable outputs.

Another important takeaway was the importance of **optimizing both cost and performance** when working with AI technologies. Understanding the strengths and limitations of AI tools enables developers to choose the most appropriate solution for each task and use cloud resources more efficiently.
