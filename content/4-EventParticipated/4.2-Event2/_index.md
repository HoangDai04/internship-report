# Event Report

### Key Topics

#### Enterprise Multi-Agent Systems: A Startup Credit Assessment Case Study

* **Speaker:** Vy Lam

#### Transitioning to Modern Application Architecture – Microservices Architecture

### Key Takeaways

The event provided valuable insights into the design and deployment of enterprise AI systems. One of the most important lessons was the principle of **"Divide and Conquer"**, where complex tasks are assigned to specialized AI agents to improve efficiency and accuracy.

Another key takeaway was the importance of implementing **checks and balances** within AI systems to ensure reliability, transparency, and accountability. In enterprise environments, an AI solution should not only function correctly but also operate **securely, reliably, and sustainably**.

In addition, the speakers emphasized that when proposing AI initiatives to business leaders, technical advantages alone are not sufficient. Demonstrating **Return on Investment (ROI)** through measurable business outcomes is essential for gaining stakeholder support.
