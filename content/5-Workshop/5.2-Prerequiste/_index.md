### Workshop Product Notes

During the project implementation, the team planned to deploy the system on the AWS platform to simulate a real-world production environment. However, due to limitations in available AWS resources and deployment time, the AWS deployment version could not be fully completed according to the original plan.

In addition, most of the analysis, system design, development, and completion process was carried out individually by me. During the final stage of the project, in order to ensure the reporting schedule and provide a working product for demonstration, I developed and deployed a backup version of the system.

The backup version was successfully deployed on the **Vercel** platform and can be accessed at:

**Backup Demo:** https://gym-management-system-mauve-nine.vercel.app/

This version still provides all the main functionalities of the system, including:

* User login and authentication.
* Member management.
* Trainer management.
* Membership package management.
* Payment management.
* Attendance management.
* Overview dashboard and statistics.

This version is used to demonstrate and present the system's features during the final report presentation.
